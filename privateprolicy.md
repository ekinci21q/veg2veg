# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 21, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Veg2Veg ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using Veg2Veg, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Business Account Information**: Business name, manager name, email address, phone number
- **Authentication Credentials**: Email address and encrypted password for login
- **Profile Information**: Account manager details, business profile image
- **Contact Information**: Phone numbers and email addresses for communication purposes

### 2. Order and Transaction Data

- **Order History**: Products ordered, quantities, delivery preferences, order dates
- **Credit Balance**: Available credits, transaction history, expiry information
- **Cart Information**: Items in cart, selected variants, quantities
- **Delivery Details**: Preferred delivery time slots, delivery addresses

### 3. Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information, including:

- **Device Identifiers**: Unique device ID, device model, manufacturer
- **Operating System**: OS version, platform type (iOS/Android)
- **App Version**: Application version and build number

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### 1. Service Delivery
- Process and fulfill orders
- Manage credit balances and transactions
- Provide customer support
- Send order confirmations and delivery updates

### 2. Account Management
- Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions
- Connect you with your dedicated account manager
- Manage business profiles and preferences

### 3. App Functionality
- Personalize your experience
- Remember cart items and preferences
- Enable product search and filtering
- Process sourcing requests

### 4. Analytics and Improvements
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our App

### 5. Communication
- Respond to inquiries and support requests
- Provide order status updates

### 1. Service Providers
We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:
- Hosting and infrastructure

### 2. Business Partners

## Data Storage and Security

### 1. Local Storage
The App stores certain data locally on your device:
- Cart items (using SharedPreferences)
- Order history (using Hive local database)
- Session tokens
- User preferences

### 2. Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted password storage
- Secure HTTPS connections for all API calls
- Device-level security checks (root/jailbreak detection)
- Regular security audits

### 3. Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Account Deletion**: Upon request, we will delete your personal information within 30 days

## Your Rights and Choices

### 1. Access and Update
- View and update your business profile information within the App
- Access your complete order history
- Review your credit balance and transaction details

### 2. Data Deletion
You have the right to:
- Clear your cart at any time
- Delete local order history by clearing app data
- Request deletion of your account by contacting support at ekinci21q@outlook.com

### 3. Communication Preferences
- Opt out of promotional emails

### 4. Do Not Track
We currently do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers, as the App operates as a native mobile application.
